Multidisciplinary programmer with a passion for web and software development

 

I am an experienced systems administrator with a demonstrated history of proposing solutions to systems design and architecture and translating the design into data-center-ready implementation. Moreover, systems administration puts high demands on communication and time management which are not only skills that I have developed but also alleviated these constraints through automating processes via scripting languages. I graduated Stevens Institute of Technology in 2013 with a B.S. in Computer Science and went on to work in Systems Administration for the past five years. In 2018 I took a look at how far web development has come and realized that it was time to reinvest in my abilities as a developer, so I enrolled in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp for Full Stack Development, and I haven't looked back. I enjoy using my background in systems to make informed decisions on software development knowing fully the impact it has on websites, applications, and ultimately the end-user experience.
